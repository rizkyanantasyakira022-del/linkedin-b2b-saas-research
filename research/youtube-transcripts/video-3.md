# Video: Demand Generation vs Lead Generation Explained
Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qw6K8d9P0xY (Refine Labs-style discussion content)
Creator: B2B Marketing Insights Channel
Topic: Demand Generation Strategy

## Key Takeaways
- Demand generation focuses on building audience trust over time
- Lead generation is short-term and conversion-focused
- Modern SaaS companies are shifting toward demand-first strategies
