# Gia Laudi — CUSTOMER INTELLIGENCE SYSTEMS

Core Theme
GTM is evolving into continuous customer intelligence systems.

Key Insights

- AI transforms customer research into persistent intelligence layer
- Voice of customer becomes always-on input to GTM decisions

System Interpretation
- Customer understanding is becoming real-time infrastructure, not periodic research.

Core Principle
- The best GTM systems continuously learn from customer reality.
