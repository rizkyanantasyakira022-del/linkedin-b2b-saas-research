## CHRIS WALKER — DEMAND GEN & TRUST COLLAPSE SYSTEMS

Core Theme
- AI increases noise in marketing systems, forcing a shift toward trust and category clarity.

Key Insights
- AI-generated content increases signal degradation across platforms
- Category creation is often misinterpreted as failure before success
- High-level decisions often diverge from conventional analytical frameworks

System Interpretation
- Demand generation is becoming a trust + clarity optimization problem in increasingly noisy digital environments.

Core Principle
- In high-noise environments, clarity and trust become the primary conversion drivers.
