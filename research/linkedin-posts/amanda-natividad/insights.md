## AMANDA NATIVIDAD — DISTRIBUTION & AI VISIBILITY SYSTEMS

Core Theme
- Content success is now determined by distribution and AI visibility, not volume.

Key Insights
- Marketing strategy begins with distribution constraints, not content production
- SEO is evolving into AI answer engine optimization (AEO)
- Content saturation reduces differentiation unless distribution is intentionally designed

System Interpretation
- Organic growth is transitioning from content creation to visibility engineering across both search engines and AI systems.

Core Principle
- If AI can generate it, distribution determines its value.
