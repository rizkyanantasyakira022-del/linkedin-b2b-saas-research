## ERIC SIU — AI ORG TRANSFORMATION SYSTEMS

Core Theme
- Marketing is evolving into closed-loop intelligence systems.

Key Insights
- AI loops replace manual prompting workflows
- Future orgs will be built on shared intelligence layers (“Company Brain”)
- Open-loop AI usage is inefficient compared to autonomous systems

System Interpretation
- Companies are shifting from tool usage to intelligence infrastructure.

Core Principle
- The advantage is not AI usage — it is AI system design.
