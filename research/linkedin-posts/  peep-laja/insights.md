# Peep Laja — TRUST & CONVERSION SYSTEMS

Core Theme
- Trust is the primary constraint in modern SaaS conversion.

Key Insights
- Buyers no longer trust vendor claims by default
- Marketing effectiveness is limited by credibility gap, not messaging quality
- GTM failures often come from misaligned pressure strategies

System Interpretation
- Marketing is shifting from persuasion to verification infrastructure.

Core Principle
- Trust is the gating function of all SaaS revenue.
