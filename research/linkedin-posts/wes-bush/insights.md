## Wes Bush — SPEED-TO-VALUE & ACTIVATION SYSTEMS

Core Theme
Speed-to-value is the new competitive moat in SaaS.

Key Insights
- SaaS success depends on critical activation milestones
- AI reduces differentiation in product capability
- Time-to-value is now a primary growth lever

System Interpretation
Winning SaaS companies optimize the gap between signup and first meaningful value.

Core Principle
- Speed is now a structural advantage, not an optimization metric.
